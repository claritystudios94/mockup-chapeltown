Treatments

Everything your family needs

From NHS check-ups for the kids to cosmetic treatments for you — we offer a full range of care under one roof in Chapeltown.

NHS Available
🏥

NHS Dental Treatment

Comprehensive NHS care including examinations, fillings, extractions and hygiene. We're proud to continue offering NHS treatment to our registered patients and their families.

✨

Teeth Whitening

Professional Philips Zoom whitening for a brighter, more confident smile. Results in as little as one appointment — or take-home trays at your convenience.

📐

Orthodontics

Straighten your smile discreetly with Invisalign. For more complex cases, we refer to trusted local specialists. NHS orthodontics available for children.

💎

Veneers

Porcelain or composite veneers to reshape, resize, or resurface teeth. A transformative treatment that can be completed in just two appointments.

🔗

Crowns & Bridges

Restore damaged or missing teeth with solutions that blend naturally with your smile. Durable, long-lasting, and designed to match your existing teeth.

🧹

Hygiene & Prevention

Regular scale and polish, gum disease treatment, and personalised advice to keep your smile healthy between visits.
